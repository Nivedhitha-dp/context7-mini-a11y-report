How to Use ▼

Using this expand/collapse widget is incredibly simple. Just click on any header to expand or collapse its content.

Steps:

  • Click the header to reveal hidden content
  • Click again to collapse the section
  • Multiple sections can be open simultaneously
  • Customize colors and styles to match your brand

The widget automatically handles the animation states and provides smooth transitions between collapsed and expanded states.
