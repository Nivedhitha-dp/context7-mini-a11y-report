Accessibility means designing and building products so that people with disabilities can perceive, understand, navigate, and interact with them. It covers visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive needs.

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) provide a shared international standard. Meeting WCAG 2.1 AA is both a legal requirement in many jurisdictions and the right thing to do for your users.

Keyboard users navigate with Tab to reach interactive elements and use Enter or Space to activate buttons. Every focusable element must have a visible focus indicator so users always know where they are on the page.
