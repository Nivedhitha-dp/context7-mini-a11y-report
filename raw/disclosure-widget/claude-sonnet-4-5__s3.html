About This Widget

Widget 3: FAQ Section

How does this work?

The widget uses a combination of CSS classes and JavaScript to toggle between expanded and collapsed states.

When collapsed, the content has zero max-height and zero opacity. When expanded, it transitions to its full height with full opacity.

This creates a smooth, professional-looking animation effect.
