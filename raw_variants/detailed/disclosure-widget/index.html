Common Questions

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items in their original condition. Simply bring your receipt or order confirmation to any of our locations, or start a return online through your account dashboard.

Personalised or digital items are not eligible for return unless they arrive damaged or defective.

Standard shipping typically takes 3–5 business days within the continental United States. Expedited (1–2 business days) and overnight options are available at checkout.

International orders generally arrive within 7–14 business days, depending on the destination country and customs processing times.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of being placed, provided they have not yet entered the fulfilment process. To make changes, contact our support team as soon as possible via live chat or by calling the number on your order confirmation.

Once an order has been dispatched, it can no longer be cancelled, but you can initiate a return once it arrives.

Price-match requests can be submitted through our website or at any physical location. Marketplace sellers and auction sites are not eligible for price-matching.
