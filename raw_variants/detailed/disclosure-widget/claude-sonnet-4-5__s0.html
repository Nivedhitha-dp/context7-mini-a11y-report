WCAG stands for Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. It is an internationally recognized set of standards developed by the W3C to make web content more accessible.

WCAG 2.2 Level AA is the commonly referenced standard for accessibility compliance, covering principles of perceivability, operability, understandability, and robustness.
