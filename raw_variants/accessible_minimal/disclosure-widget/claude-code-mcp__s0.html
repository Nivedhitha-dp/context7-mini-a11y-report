WCAG stands for Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. They are published by the W3C and define how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

WCAG 2.1 Level AA is the most widely adopted accessibility standard, referenced in legal frameworks around the world.

Semantic HTML communicates meaning to browsers, assistive technologies, and search engines. Elements like <button>, <nav>, and <main> convey their purpose without extra ARIA.

All interactive elements must be reachable and operable by keyboard alone. This section starts expanded to show default open state.

Use Tab to move between focusable elements and Enter or Space to activate buttons.
